Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Brittany Parker.

I founded Parker Tech Lab with a simple mission: to help businesses create digital experiences that are both beautiful and functional. Too often, companies have to choose between stunning design and practical functionality. I believe you deserve both.

With a background spanning web development, automation, and AI integration, I bring a unique technical perspective to every project. But beyond the code, I'm passionate about understanding your business goals and crafting solutions that drive real results.

When I'm not building websites, you'll find me exploring new AI technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or mentoring aspiring developers. I believe in continuous learning and pushing the boundaries of what's possible on the web.
