FAQ

Common questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Most website projects take 3-6 weeks from kickoff to launch, depending on scope and complexity. Landing pages can be completed in 1-2 weeks, while larger custom platforms may take 8-12 weeks.

Do you work with businesses outside the US?

Yes! We work with clients worldwide. All communication and meetings are conducted remotely via video call, email, and project management tools.

What information do you need to get started?

We start with a discovery call to understand your goals, audience, and requirements. After that, we'll provide a detailed proposal and project plan before any work begins.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment structures. Most projects follow a 50% deposit, 50% on completion model, but we can discuss milestone-based payments for larger projects.
